Overview

Problem

Parking in dense, urban areas can be a stressful, frustrating experience. Factors like the lengthy time spent looking for a spot, expensive parking rates, and overall safety concerns can lead to a negative experience with parking.

Solution

A mobile valet service that can park and retrieve your car for you in urban areas or at events where parking can be tough. Payment is taken through the app and you never need cash.

Competitor Analisys

Park Mobile

  • You can only park in designated zones around specific cities
  • Pre plan options for events only
  • Our users reported a difficult checkout process

My Park

  • Helps you schedule parking at large malls , and offers rates when booking, but there are very few places you can use it.
  • Offers directions to your parking spot and takes all credit cards.

Spot Hero

  • Map available with rates, provides address and satellite view of parking spot.
  • Allows for a detailed, tiered search
  • Provides parking lot type and amenities, hours and directions, and "things you should know
  • Provides parking options and rates specific to date and time
  • Has a benefits program and allows for personal and business account options.

Features

  • Park now at a mobile valet Hotspot anywhere in urban areas.
  • Pre-plan and schedule your parking with a mobile valet.
  • Shows you rates at the Hotspot or mobile valet before you park.
  • Allows you to pay through the app using all major credit cards and offers connection with Venmo.
  • Navigation to your parking spot or share your parking location with a friend.

Next Steps

Based on our user and market research, we believe ParkMe would be a success in any major city where parking is scarce and traffic is an issue.

  • The next phase would entail gaining VC funding for full development of the app, including build-out of all user functions (customer, valets, and parking lot/structure owners).
  • We will roll out a beta test in three major cities: New York City, San Francisco, Los Angeles to measure demand where parking is most scarce and resolve any UX issues that still remain. 
  • Finally, the goal would be to roll-out the app in major cities across the country. Revenue would be generated via ads and obtaining a 2% service fee from our partners on every transaction.
